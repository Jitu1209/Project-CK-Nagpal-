GDSC

The Google Developer Student Club (GDSC) is a university-based community supported by Google, aimed at promoting software development and technology learning. GDSC provides students with opportunities to learn about Google technologies, participate in coding challenges, work on real-world projects, and collaborate with peers. The club encourages innovation and skill-building through a variety of technical activities.
