IEEE

The IEEE Electronics Society, also known as the IEEE Electronics Packaging Society (EPS), is a professional society within the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE). It focuses on the science, technology, and engineering aspects of electronic components, devices, circuits, and systems. The society aims to advance the state-of-the-art in electronics through technical education, research, collaboration, and industry engagement.
