Ananya

Ananya is a society dedicated to the love of literature, poetry, and creative writing. It creates a space for students interested in reading, writing, and performing poetry or prose. Ananya hosts literary events, poetry slams, book discussions, and writing workshops, fostering a community of literary enthusiasts and encouraging artistic expression through words.
